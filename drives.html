Nature Drives

Experience Hiltaba through a vehicle tour

Saint Mungo Drive

The Saint Mungo Nature Drive takes visitors off Hiltaba road and through the retired cattle yards and grazing areas through to the Pretty Point Camp Ground. The dams and water wells from Hiltaba's previous life can be observed along the way. Half way along the drive is an optional walk to the summit of Mt Saint Mungo, providing stunning 360-degree views. St Mungo Nature Drive is approximately 79 kilometres long and is an easy to moderate drive. Some sections of the Saint Mungo Drive may be suitable for conventional vehicles with good ground clearance, but the lookout section should only be completed by 4WD vehicles. Before commencing the Nature Drive ensure you are prepared and have paid the station manager. Alpaca Map of Drive

Pine Well Drive

The Pine Well Nature Drive features magnificent views of the reserve while passing by a retired Sheep Shearing Shed, Narlaby Well and Punkey Dam. The Desert Green-Hood Orchid, only found within Hiltaba, may be seen along this track. Spectacular pipe rock formations may also be found along this drive. The drive is approximately 77 kilometres in length and an easy to moderate drive. Pine Well Nature Drive is only suitable for 4WD vehicles due to uneven terrain and exposed rocks. Tag Along Tours

When available, our station managers love to show visitors around the reserve and share their extensive knowledge. You will truly learn what make Hiltaba the jewel of the Gawler Ranges and hear the fascinating history of our property.
