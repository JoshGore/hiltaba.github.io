Itinerary

Skip the crowds and take the road less trodden! The detour to Hiltaba runs parallel to the Eyre Highway, leaving at Kimba and joining again at Wirrulla. This trip is well worth your time, also providing access to the Gawler Ranges National Park and Mt Isa Station. As reception is often patchy through this area we have provided an Alpaca Maps itinerary. You can download this and use it on the go!

Hiltaba Nature Drives

We have also provided itineraries for both Nature Drives at Hiltaba. Download these if you plan to do one of the drives for mobile based track instructions and notes.

Other Offline Map Options

The application "OsmAND" (Apple | Android) will show most of the walking and driving tracks around the Hiltaba area. You can also download areas in Google Maps for offline use.
