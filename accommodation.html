Staying at Hiltaba

Sleep in a bed or under the stars!

Accommodation Options

We have several accommodation buildings available totalling 27 beds. The Governess and Jackaroo Cottages are built around historic cottages near the main homestead. These cottages sleep five people each. The restored Shearers Quarters around a kilometre from the homestead has 17 beds as well as a fully equiped kitchen, a large common room, and a dedicated research room. We also have two outdoor camping areas, one outside the Shearers Quarters and the other at Pretty Point. Showers are available to camping parties for a small fee.

How to Book Accommodation

Please fill out our booking form. If you have any queries you can reach us at reservations@nfsa.org.au
Please note there will be modest price increases for 2019; please contact the office for details. Accommodation and tours are subject to operational requirements (researchers on site are given priority).
